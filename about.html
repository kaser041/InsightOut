What is *project name*?

*project name* is an interactive visualization application which helps people who want to study soon in their decision making.

The application visualizes different aspects of the three study programs Komedia, Applied Computer Science (AI) and Computer Engineering (ISE) of university duisburg-essen.

Users can compare study program information (eg. study contents) as well as opinions and evaluations by students of the respective study programs.
